Commercial vs. Bespoke Software

The market is rich in ready-made software products intended to manage your Supply Chain, Transportation, Warehouse, or Customers. But the solution designed for everybody is not supposed to satisfy all the needs of your particular business. By using a third-party software solution, you're not saving resources – you're postponing necessary further investments.
